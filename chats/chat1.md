## User

Claude Design Prompt - Christian Family Church Full Site
Design a complete multi-page website prototype for Christian Family Church (CFC) (www.christianfamilychurch.co.za), a multi-campus church based in Johannesburg, South Africa. The site is being rebuilt from Wix into a Next.js platform with Payload CMS. Use Church of the Highlands (churchofthehighlands.com) as the primary design reference for layout philosophy, spacing, visual rhythm, and navigation structure. Every page should feel part of the same design system, not designed in isolation.

Brand Context

Name: Christian Family Church (CFC)
Short name used throughout: CFC
Tagline: Living Life Together
Purpose statement: Know God. Find Freedom. Discover Purpose. Make a Difference.
The church has multiple campuses across Johannesburg with more planned. Campus is a first-class concept across the entire site, not an afterthought.
Logo is a cross mark paired with a wordmark. Treat it cleanly in the nav with generous spacing.
Imagery placeholders should reflect real photography of people, worship, community, and church spaces. No stock photo aesthetics, no illustration.


Design System - Apply Across Every Page
Typography: Modern sans-serif pairing. Heavy display weight for headings, light for body. Warm but confident. Avoid ALL CAPS for body content. Short punchy headings only.
Colours:

Primary: Deep charcoal or dark navy for navbar, footer, and dark sections
Secondary: White and light grey for content sections
Accent: A single warm accent for CTAs, links, and highlights. Not generic blue. Consider deep gold, warm teal, or a considered brand colour.
Alternate dark and light sections as the page scrolls to create rhythm without relying on text.

Spacing: Generous. Let content breathe. Do not crowd sections together.
Buttons: Pill-style or clean outlined CTAs. Not heavy filled rectangles on every section.
Cards: Large image, short label above heading, minimal or no body text. 2 or 3 per row maximum.
Images: All placeholder images should be full-bleed, portrait or landscape photography, with subtle dark overlays where text sits on top.
Navigation: Dark background. Logo left-aligned. Right-aligned nav items: New to CFC, About, Campuses, Watch, Give, Sign In. On mobile, collapse to a hamburger with a full-screen slide-in menu.
Footer: Dark background. CFC logo. Column links for About, Connect, Watch, Ministries. Campus list. Social icons: Facebook, Instagram, YouTube. Copyright and Privacy Policy link.

Mood Across the Entire Site
Every person landing on any page of this site may be at a different point in their life. Some are visiting a church for the first time, some are long-time members, some are returning after years away. The design should consistently feel like a welcome, not a brochure. Warm, spacious, human, real. Images of actual people in community should anchor every section. The design earns trust through restraint, not through filling every pixel with content.
